Important Reminders:

The class will follow David Borthwick's Introduction to Partial Differential Equations (DOI: https://doi.org/10.1007/978-3-319-48936-0) available from the UCB Library Proxy or directly from Springer with access granted by the UC system. Topics will include:

  • Introduction; review of calculus and ordinary differential equations
  • Method of Characteristics and Conservation Equations
  • The Wave Equation
  • Separation of Variables for the Wave and Laplace Equations
  • The Heat Equation
  • An introduction to function spaces
  • Fourier Series applications to PDEs
  • The maximum principle for the Laplace Equation
  • Calculus of Variations
